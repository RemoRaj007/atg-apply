How it works

Four steps. Real people at every one.

01

Tell us about you

Complete a guided intake — experience, target roles, documents. About 12 minutes.

02

We research & score

Your team finds roles and rates each one with a clear fit score and an honest reason.

03

You approve

Nothing is sent without your say-so. Approve the roles you like, skip the rest.

04

We apply & prove it

We tailor, QC and submit — then log proof of every application to your dashboard.

Not a bot. A team.
